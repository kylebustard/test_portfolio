About me.

I am a web application developer. I use Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, RSpec, Bootstrap, GitHub, and Mac OS X.

I am very new to developing, but my newly discovered passion for the industry and learning new technologies has quickly turned into an obsession. As a child, my favorite school subject was history. In college I was a double major student of philosophy and sociology. I am fascinated by how small ideas can evolve into significant phenomenons and even institutions.

I was around computer science students in some of my philosophy and logic courses, but I never had an affinity for technology (in fact I didn't ever learn how to type!). At the urging of a friend, I gave coding a chance and fell in love with it. I soon enrolled in a full-time code immersion course and devoured all learning opportunities I could find. My methodology was practical and immersive: I was teaching myself how to type while also learning to code. I began going to meetups and touring local tech companies weekly. I listen to tech podcasts every time I drive my car. I have a neat bookmark folder system in my browser—but I still manage to keep nearly twenty tabs open to documentation and code-related blogs! I've instructed 3 different kids coding classes, and I've been a gateway/mentor for a couple of friends who've started coding.

I've thrived in the high-paced learning environment of this code immersion course. In just a matter of weeks I've learned how to program, learned cool technologies, built blogs, e-commerce, and social media apps. I hope to keep learning and make my own contributions to the tech community!
